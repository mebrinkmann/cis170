Heading Tags

Heading tags represent topics and can go from h1 to h6.

H1 represents the most important content. H2 is a subtopic of H1. H3 is a subtopic of H2.

Formatting Tags

The strong tag makes sure the text is marked as important.

The b tag is used to make the text bold, but not important (use only if your design requires something to look bold, but it's not as important)

The em tag marks texts with emphasis.
